Why we are here

We exist to help others.

In our industry, "helpful" is a marketing word. Service is a transaction. The technician is a stranger who leaves before you remember his name. We don't accept any of that.

Our vision is to become the best, most helpful provider of home preservation services across the country. Not the biggest. Not the cheapest. The most helpful. Helpful is a responsibility, and we are built to carry it.

We help our employees. Our neighbors. The communities we serve. We do it by inspiring one another, redefining what great service looks like, and spreading good by doing good every day.

Our communities are stronger because Ace Hardware Home Services stays true to this mission.

We answer to three groups

Three groups. One mission.

Helping is not a department. It is the reason we are here. Our purpose lives in three groups, and every member of our team is responsible for all three.

01 / The A-Team

We help our employees.

When we hire someone, we are inviting them to join our A-Team. An eclectic group of amazing individuals who authentically show up for one another every day. Working in an encouraging environment with like-minded people, our employees thrive. As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another.

It is our duty to offer people a meaningful and fulfilling life, skillfully working with their head and their hands.
02 / Our Neighbors

We help our customers.

We show up prepared, tell the truth, and stand behind our work. Through our Red Bow Promise, we choose long-term trust over short-term gain and always do what's right, even when it's harder.

We treat customers with integrity. No shortcuts, no surprises.
03 / Our Communities

We help our communities.

We are called not to curse the darkness, but to shine a light in it. We give our time, money, and energy to make a lasting change for those who need it. We have an obligation to better the community we are a part of and make our world a better place.

A spirit of giving is an essential trait. We are here to positively impact the world.
What we promise
The Red Bow Promise is the standard we hold ourselves to.

Our customers can expect us to show up on time, be upfront on price, do it right, and leave their home better than we found it.

We will lose the quarter to win the quarter century. That is the Red Bow.

What we measure ourselves by

Seven values. Non-negotiable.

These are not aspirations or slogans. They are the behaviors our neighbors, our partners, and our teammates experience consistently.

People who align with them succeed here.
People who do not, do not last.

01

Winning

Raving fans, no mediocrity

We create raving fans of our customers and each other. We don't tolerate mediocrity. We do what's right, not what's easy, and we go beyond what's expected. What others call "extra," we call standard.

02

Excellence

The bar, raised every day

Pricing confirmed before any work begins. The right tech, with the right time, the same day when possible. No pressure tactics. No foot-in-the-door. No "let me check with my manager" theater. Every interaction, every home, every day.

03

Love

Our highest value

This isn't a job. It's a calling. The interaction with our team member might be the best moment in someone's whole day, and we know it. We hire for that. Love is the highest value here. We don't apologize for it.

04

Integrity

No moral compromises

Skills can be taught. Integrity can't. So we hire for work ethic and values first, and train the rest. We do what we say. We take ownership when we miss. We don't make excuses, and we don't tolerate them.

05

Gratitude

Attitude shapes impact

Not everyone gets to do work that actually makes a difference in someone's life. We do, and we don't take that lightly. We say thank you often, not because it's expected, but because it's deserved. People get seen here.

06

Humility

Quiet confidence

Listen before speaking. Ask questions before making assumptions. Give credit before taking it. Titles don't define worth here. Every role matters, every voice counts. We focus on doing what is right, not on being right.

07

Teamwork

We show up for each other

We step in, pitch in, and get it done...together. No one gets left behind, and no job is someone else's problem.

Building Something That Lasts

We don't think quarters.
We think quarter centuries.

For generations, Ace Hardware retailers have earned trust by showing up, doing the right thing, and serving their communities with pride. Ace Hardware Home Services extends that same trust from the aisle into the home.

Most home services companies are built to be sold. We are built to outlast the founders' grandchildren. Privately held. Values driven. No quarterly earnings call. No five-year exit timeline. No buyer in mind.

We are not just fixing plumbing, HVAC, or electrical systems. We are protecting homes. We are serving families. We are building meaningful careers in the skilled trades. We are strengthening the communities we serve.

Helpful is not a slogan. It is a responsibility. Every community we serve should be better because we are there.
